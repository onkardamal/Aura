About Aura

Aura understands your cognitive and emotional state and dynamically adapts the digital interface to create a safer, more accessible, and healthier online experience.

Try typing something positive or negative and watch how the interface responds!

Available Modes:

  • Study Mode: Optimizes the interface for learning with reduced distractions and enhanced focus.
  • Read Mode: Improves readability with optimal font size, line spacing, and contrast.
  • Focus Mode: Minimizes distractions and provides timers for deep work sessions.
